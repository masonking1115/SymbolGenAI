### W2. MCP4728 EEPROM default codes must be 0xFFF to keep PMOS off at POR

**Component(s):** U40 (sheet: `bias`)
**Requirement:** design_requirements.md:24,91
**Datasheet:** MCP4728 22187E §4.2 Table 4-2, §4.4
**Observed:** design_requirements.md:91 documents this. Virgin parts ship with code 0x000 (V_OUT=0V) → full-scale bias current at POR.
**Impact:** Virgin MCP4728 → DAC=0V → PMOS fully on → ~646 µA into BIAS0/1 → potential DUT damage.
**Fix:** Provision MCP4728 EEPROM (all 4 channels = 0xFFF) before installing on a production board.
<sub>rule: `SEM_MCP4728_EEPROM_DEFAULT` · subject: `U40` · autofix: `manual`</sub>

### W3. MCP4728 VREF must be programmed to external (VDD) — virgin part defaults to internal 2.048V

**Component(s):** U40 (sheet: `bias`)
**Requirement:** design_requirements.md:20,91
**Datasheet:** MCP4728 22187E §4.5, Table 4-3
**Observed:** design_requirements.md:91 documents the assembly note. No schematic-level way to enforce — VREF source is an EEPROM bit on the part itself.
